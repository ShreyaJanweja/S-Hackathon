Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the hackathon?

Visit our registration page and fill out the form. Registration is free and open to all eligible participants.

What are the eligibility criteria?

Participants must be students or recent graduates. Teams can have 2-4 members. Check our website for full details.

Is there a participation fee?

No, participation is completely free. We provide meals, swags, and all necessary resources.

Can I participate remotely?

This is an in-person event at Chitkara University. Remote participation is not available.
