About S-Hackathon

S-Hackathon 2025 is not just another coding event; it’s a 24-hour marathon of passion, purpose, and potential. Hosted at Chitkara University, Punjab, the hackathon brings together innovators, dreamers, and doers from all corners of the country to shape the future with code. With expert mentorship, vibrant themes, and a spirit of collaboration, S-Hackathon is your launchpad to turn ideas into world-changing solutions.
